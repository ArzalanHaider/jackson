The Shadow of Ging Pei

From the author of The Journey Home comes a powerful and inspiring new book, The Shadow of Ging Pei. Ronald Jackson shares a deeply personal and spiritual journey of overcoming adversity, finding faith, and embracing transformation.

Born and raised in South Jamaica, Queens, New York, Jackson faced significant challenges, including dropping out of school and battling Multiple Sclerosis. Despite these hardships, he refused to be defined by his circumstances. Through unwavering faith and determination, he rebuilt his life-becoming an ordained minister, a successful small business owner, and a scholar in computer technology and forensics.

The Journey Home

In The Journey Home, the author invites readers on a profound spiritual exploration of God's love, forgiveness, and the path to salvation. Drawing from Scripture, personal experiences, and heartfelt testimonies, this book offers a roadmap for anyone seeking to reconnect with God. It shares the transformative power of faith, offering hope, healing, and the promise of redemption. Aimed at both seasoned believers and those new to their spiritual journey, this work provides inspiration and practical guidance on how to live a life filled with grace and purpose
